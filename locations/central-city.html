Central City

The capital of Zhery and one of the densest early hubs in the game, with item NPCs, city services, casino rewards, and rooftop encounters.

Berries

  • 2 Oran Berries
  • 1 Chesto Berry
  • 1 Razz Berry on the rooftop of the building beside the Trainer House

Pokemart Special Stock

  • TM Return - 10,000
  • Heal Ball - 300
  • Luxury Ball - 1,000
  • Nest Ball - 1,000

Important Finds

  • Ultra Ball from the old man near Route 401 after getting the Pokedex
  • Global Terminal beside the train station for GTS online trade services
  • Bulk Up from the man training with Croagunk beside Zhery’s Radio-TV station
  • Coin Case from the clown in the right house in front of the Pokemon Center
  • Trainer House in the purple-colored building in front of the Coin Case house
  • Gen 2 starter reward from the casino 15-picture bonus streak

Encounters & Notes
